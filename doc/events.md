
## Event Priority


We are assuming the events behave in the following way

### Mouse move

1. mouseover
2. mousemove
3. mouseout

### Mouse press

1. mousedown
2. change (on focused input)
3. blur (on focused element)
4. focus
5. mouseup
6. click
7. dblclick

### Key press

1. keydown
2. change (on focused input)
3. blur (on focused element)
4. focus
5. keypress
6. keyup (on focused element)
